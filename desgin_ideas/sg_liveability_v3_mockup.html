📖 What is this app?

This dashboard helps you decide whether a Singapore district is worth moving into if it has no MRT station nearby. It analyses real-time bus and taxi data from LTA (Land Transport Authority) and gives each district a connectivity score from 0 to 100. Higher score = easier to get around without MRT.

🏆 District Connectivity Score (0–100)

ScoreMeaning
75–100Well connected — comfortable without MRT
50–74Moderate — manageable with some planning
0–49Poor — transit friction is high

Bus 50% (main transport) · Taxi 30% (backup) · Friction −20% (penalises hard-to-get-taxi areas)

🚕 Taxi Metrics

Live Taxis — what does this number mean?▾
The exact number of taxis available in the district right now, from LTA's API, updated every 60 seconds.
Taxi Flux — what does +/- mean?▾
Flux = current taxi count minus the count 60 seconds ago. +5 means supply increasing, −8 means taxis are leaving the area.
Estimated Pickups — how do we detect them?▾
We compare snapshots 60 seconds apart. A 20m buffer is drawn around every taxi; any taxi from the old snapshot that falls outside all buffers is treated as picked up. This is the Taxi Disappearance Engine.
Friction Index — what does 0.425 mean?▾
Friction = Estimated Pickups ÷ Current Taxis. 0.0–0.2 = low demand, 0.2–0.5 = moderate, 0.5–1.0 = high demand (taxis disappearing fast).
Taxi Stability Score — what is this?▾
Measures how consistent taxi supply is over the last 15 minutes, using Coefficient of Variation (CV). Score 87/100 means very predictable supply.

🚌 Bus Metrics

Bus Frequency Score — how is this calculated?▾
Based on average headway (gap between buses). ≤2min = 100/100 excellent, ~8min = ~78/100 good (typical SG), ~15min = ~54/100 moderate, ≥30min = 0/100 poor.
Route Redundancy — why does it matter?▾
Number of distinct bus services reachable from the district. More routes = more resilience if one service is delayed or cancelled.

📐 ML Model Metrics

MAE — Mean Absolute Error▾
How many taxis our prediction is off by on average. 0–3 excellent, 3–6 good, 6–10 OK, 10+ poor. Our model: +30min MAE 3.08, +60min 3.92, +2hr 5.03.
Why does accuracy get worse further out?▾
Normal and expected — more can change (rain, events, rush hour ending) and small errors compound over longer horizons. Like weather forecasting.
